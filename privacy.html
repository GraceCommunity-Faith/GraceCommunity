Privacy Policy

App: Grace Community Church
Effective Date: July 2026

Summary: The Grace Community Church app does not require user accounts and does not collect personal data automatically. If you contact us by email or through a linked web form, we use the information you provide only to respond to your inquiry or prayer request.

Information We Collect

The Grace Community Church app does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information automatically.

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Photos, files, or uploads
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers

Voluntary Contact

If you choose to contact us through email links or submit a contact or prayer request form linked from the app, you may voluntarily provide personal information such as your name, email address, or prayer request. This information is used only to respond to your inquiry or prayer request and is not shared with third parties except as necessary to distribute prayer requests to our authorized church prayer team.

External Links

The app contains links to external websites and services, including YouTube, Facebook, online giving, and our church website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites or services.

Children's Privacy

Grace Community Church does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Analytics and Tracking

The Grace Community Church app does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or collect user behavior for marketing purposes.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
